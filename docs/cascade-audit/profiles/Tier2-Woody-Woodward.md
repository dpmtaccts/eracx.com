## Key Finding
Woody is doing the job of Chief Coaching Officer beautifully on LinkedIn — the coach community knows him, engages with him, and surfaces his content — and that is the problem; ninety percent of his audience is coaches and coaching consultants, which is not the audience that decides BetterUp purchases, and the energy he puts into reposting BetterUp company-page content twelve times in ninety days is being eaten by the algorithm penalty on brand-handle reshares. The Sasimar panel post is the model: long, substantive, names twenty-five coach peers, and the engagement quality is the highest of any C-suite voice we have analyzed when measured by depth-of-thread. Same form, different audience — directed at CHROs — would be transformative.

## Three Things
1. **Cut the BetterUp page reposts in half.** Twelve in ninety days is excessive; six is plenty. The slot replaced by substantive engagement on a CHRO post is worth more.
2. **One coach-network-to-buyer translation post per month.** Take the same names-twenty-coaches form he already uses and add the buyer-relevant payoff: "Here is what twenty-five coaches at the Sasimar All-Pro Project agreed on about why AI-coaching ROI lands at the manager-effectiveness layer for a CHRO defending L&D budget."
3. **Activate the ICF community as the Coach Ambassador program.** Woody is the natural lead for this; he has the standing in the community. Convert his existing repost behavior into a structured ambassador content program with two thousand coaches participating, not him alone.
