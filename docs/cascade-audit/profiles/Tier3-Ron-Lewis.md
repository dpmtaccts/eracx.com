## Key Finding
Ron is the clearest example in the dataset of the algorithm-paradox the audit is built around: he posts roughly four times a week, he gets almost no algorithmic reach because eleven of every twelve posts is a brand-handle repost, and the one post he writes himself is a consulting-firm recruiting announcement. The Vas Narasimhan repost is illustrative — it is Novartis-CEO-level content that BetterUp's company page already reaches everyone with, and Ron's repost adds nothing while consuming his daily distribution credit.
