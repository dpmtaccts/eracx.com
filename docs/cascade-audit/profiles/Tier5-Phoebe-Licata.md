## Key Finding
Phoebe is consistent without being substantive; her cadence is among the more reliable in Tier 5, but the content mix is dominated by Uplift event promotion and personal milestones, with the only seller-anchored original post in ninety days being a certification announcement. The 4-year anniversary post is the engagement outlier because it triggers reciprocal anniversary-greetings; that signal does not transfer to her other content. The Value Selling certification post is the seed of the right form — seller-experience-anchored — and the audit's recommendation is to expand that thread into a regular series ("here is what value-selling discipline is teaching me about how my buyers respond to L&D pitches").

1. **One Value-Selling-applied seller-POV post per month.** "Here is the question the framework taught me to ask in last week's discovery call, and what the CHRO answered." Specific. Useful.
2. **Drop the BetterUp page reposts in half.** They are eating the slot her own voice could fill.
3. **Engage substantively on the CHRO comments she already has in her network.** The relationships are there; she does not currently use them for content distribution.
