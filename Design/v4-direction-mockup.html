§04 HOW IT WORKS
3 PHASES · 9 STAGES NON-LINEAR

The
loop runs.

Nine stages, three phases. The loop is not a sequence. Stages fire in parallel. A signal in Detect can trigger Reach, which triggers a fresh round of Score, which feeds Expand. Read it as a system, not a timeline.

01
Connect
Stage 01
Detect
Buyer signal fires. Hiring, funding, traffic, intent.
Stage 02
Enrich
Account context built. Stack, headcount, exec map.
Stage 03
Score
FRVRD applied. Account ranked against the book.
02
Trust
Stage 04
Reach
Right channel, right voice, right moment. Not blast.
Stage 05
Respond
Signal triggers next action. Within the hour, not the week.
Stage 06
Nurture
Warmth maintained between buying windows.
03
Loyalty
Stage 07
Close
Buying window opens. Deal moves on its own warmth.
Stage 08
Expand
Same account, second purchase. Warmth compounds inside it.
Stage 09
Retain
Renewal feeds the loop back into Detect on adjacent accounts.
Loop fact 01
A signal in Stage 09 (Retain) often triggers a fresh Stage 01 (Detect) on a sister account. That's how the loop earns its name.
Loop fact 02
No stage is skippable. No stage is sequential. Every named account sits in some combination of stages at any given moment.
Loop fact 03
The system replaces 80% of activity that doesn't generate warmth. Reps spend their time on accounts that are actually moving.
